Tempeh Goreng

Description

Tempeh are cooked soybeans that are fermented and pressed into a firm, dry loaf or cake or patty of varying firmness. It is the fermentation that gives the soybeans in tempeh a nutty, earthy flavor and chewy texture.

Tempeh can be grilled, baked, stir-fried, or crumbled like ground meat and used in sandwiches or tacos.

Ingredients

  • Cold uncooked tempeh
  • Water
  • Flour
  • Turmeric powder
  • Cooking oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Heat the oil in a pan
  2. Cut the tempeh into appropriate sizes
  3. Prepare the batter using flour, salt, water, and turmeric powder
  4. Coat the cut tempeh with the turmeric batter
  5. Fry the coated tempeh
  6. Take out when its golden brown and crispy
  7. Present on a plate
